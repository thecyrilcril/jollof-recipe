# Nigerian Jollof Rice Recipe

## Ingredients
- 2 cups of rice
- 4 cups of chicken stock
- 1 can of tomato paste
- 1 onion
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Wash the rice
2. Blend tomatoes and peppers
3. Fry the onions
4. Add tomato paste
5. Add rice and stock
6. Cover and cook for 40 minutes
