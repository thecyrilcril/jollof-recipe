# Bushboi01’s Classic Jollof Rice

## Ingredients
- 2 cups long grain rice
- 5 fresh tomatoes
- 2 red bell peppers
- 2 scotch bonnets
- 1 large onion
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil
- Curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions into a smooth paste.
2. Cook the mixture until water reduces.
3. Heat oil, add the cooked blend, and fry for 10 mins.
4. Add seasoning, curry, and salt. Stir well.
5. Add rice and enough water to cook it.
6. Cover and simmer on low heat until done.
7. Serve hot with fried chicken or dodo.
